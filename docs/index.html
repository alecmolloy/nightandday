The little book of event poetry

Event description

We come together to celebrate
From very early to very late

We will meet during the day
For grown up and much needed play

There will be drinking, it might get silly
(But nobody will be allowed to show their willy)

We meet again during the night
To learn to grow our inner light

The mysteries we’ll meet in shadows
Are too much to right now disclose

We’ll face deep fears for humankind
Play with the illusions in our mind

We will be brave, even in our sleep
To make the learnings thus more deep

Explore ourselves, our lives, our death
To enjoy ying and yang in our every breath

How to apply

Leave us a message of your voice
Under this whatsapp number of choice

Tell us what you imagine in those days
How we’re coming together, in which ways

Imagine up the world ahead
Explain it in a single thread

But speak your words in beautiful rhyme
So to the top of our attendance list you can climb

The cost will be low, maximum 150 quid
Which we think is fair for this event bid

Only twelve spots are there to take
So apply now and save yourself from heartache.
